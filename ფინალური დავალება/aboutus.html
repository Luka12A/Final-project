About Us

Hello! We are the founders of this company, the people who started Explore Georgia. We love Georgia and want to share its amazing beauty with you. Our journey began because we believe that travel can be more than just travel; It can be a life-changing experience. We started Explore Georgia to create special adventures in Georgia. Each of us has a personal connection to this country, and we wanted to show others why we love it so much. We work hard to make sure everything about your trip is awesome. From the places you stay to the things you do, we want it to be the best. Our goal is to introduce Georgia to tourists and Georgians.
